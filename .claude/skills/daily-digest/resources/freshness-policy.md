# Freshness Policy

Assign a freshness score to every candidate insight based on publication date:

| Score | Age |
|---|---|
| 3 | < 2 days |
| 2 | 2–7 days |
| 1 | 8–30 days |
| 0 | > 30 days |

Freshness is one input to candidate ranking. It does not gate inclusion on its own — quality rubric score determines inclusion.
