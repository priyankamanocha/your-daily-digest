# Source Credibility Rules

Assign a credibility score to every discovered source:

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 3 | Major publication, verified account, official or primary source |
| 2 | Established outlet, known author |
| 1 | Unknown but plausible, no red flags |
| 0 | Unverified, promotional, known spam |

**Exclusion rule**: Sources scoring < 2 are excluded from Insights, Anti-patterns, and Actions.
They may appear in Resources only, ranked below credible sources.
